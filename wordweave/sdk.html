Sudoku Tips & Strategies

Master the art of Sudoku with these essential tips and strategies:

  • Scanning: Look for rows, columns, and 3x3 blocks with only one missing number.
  • Marking Candidates: Pencil in potential numbers in each cell to narrow down possibilities.
  • Hidden Singles: Identify a number that appears only once as a candidate in a row, column, or block.
  • Pointing Pairs/Triples: If a number only appears as a candidate in two or three cells within a row, column, or block, you can eliminate it as a candidate from other cells in that row, column, or block.
  • X-Wing/Swordfish: Advanced techniques for expert players.

Practice regularly and gradually increase the difficulty level to enhance your skills. Remember, patience and logical thinking are key to solving Sudoku puzzles!
